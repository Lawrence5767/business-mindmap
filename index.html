Markup Matrix
By cost tier
▼

RM0.50—5 cost → 200-400% markup (sell RM2 item at RM8)

RM5—20 cost → 100-200% markup (sell RM8 item at RM18)

RM20—50 cost → 60-100% markup (sell RM30 item at RM55)

RM50—150 cost → 40-60% markup (sell RM80 item at RM120)

RM150—500 cost → 25-40% markup (sell RM250 at RM340)

RM500+ cost → 15-25% markup

Rule: cheaper items = higher % markup, expensive items = lower % but bigger RM profit per sale

🎯
Loss Leader Items
Traffic drivers
▼

Sell cheap to get customers in, upsell the rest

🟢 Engine oil change — sell branded oil near cost, free labour. Customer sees worn brake pads, buys accessories

🟢 Spark plugs — cost RM2-5, sell RM5-8. Every bike needs them regularly

🟢 Basic bulbs — cost RM1-3, sell RM3-5. Quick job = walk-in traffic

🟡 Air filters — cost RM3-8, sell RM8-15. Pairs with oil changes

🟡 Cheap brake pads — cost RM5-10, sell RM12-18. Builds trust

🟡 Chain lube — cost RM5-8, sell RM10-15. Brings customers back monthly

💡 A RM15 oil change customer typically leaves spending RM50-100+ on filters, brake pads, chain lube & accessories

🎁
Promo Freebies
▼

Free phone mount — cost RM2-3, give free with purchase > RM50

Branded keychains — near-zero cost, builds loyalty

Stickers pack — cost RM0.50, perceived value RM5+

Free installation — for parts bought in-store (labour = your time only)

Audience Analysis

👥

5 Key Audience Segments

Who rides in Puncak Alam — and how to sell to each one

🎓
UiTM Students
~40,000
▼

THE biggest audience — right next door

Profile: Age 18-25, Malay majority, tight budget, ride kapcai (Honda Wave, Yamaha LC135, Y15ZR)

Needs: Cheap replacement parts, basic maintenance items, affordable accessories to customize bikes

Spending: RM20-80 per visit, frequent small purchases

How to tackle:

→ Student discount — 5-10% off with student ID. Creates loyalty + word of mouth

→ Budget bundles — "Service Kit RM25" (oil + filter + spark plug)

→ TikTok & Instagram — students live on social media. Post bike mod tutorials, part reviews

→ WhatsApp group — "UiTM Bikers" group for deals & stock alerts

→ Semester-start promos — big push during registration week (Sep & Feb)

→ Installment for accessories — RM50+ items on 2-3 month payment via WhatsApp trust

🛵
Daily Commuters
High volume
▼

Bread-and-butter repeat customers

Profile: Age 25-45, M40 families in Hillpark/Saujana Utama/Eco Grandeur, ride daily to KL/Shah Alam via LATAR

Bikes: Honda RS150, Yamaha NVX, Honda ADV, Y15ZR — mid-range commuters

Needs: Reliability-focused — brake pads, tyres, chains, oil changes, rain gear

Spending: RM50-200 per visit, every 1-3 months

How to tackle:

→ Loyalty card — "5th oil change free" punch card

→ Early morning / evening hours — open by 8am (catch before work) & stay till 9pm

→ Quick service — oil change in 15 min, brake pads in 20 min. Speed = loyalty

→ WhatsApp reminders — "Hi bro, your next oil change is due this week"

→ Rain season promos — rain coat + visor spray bundle during monsoon (Oct-Feb)

🏭
Factory Workers
Growing fast
▼

Industrial parks expanding — this segment will grow 2-3x

Profile: Age 20-40, B40 workers at Alam Perdana Industrial, Semanja Business Park. Motorcycle is their ONLY transport

Bikes: Honda Wave, EX5, Yamaha 135LC — workhorse kapcai, high mileage

Needs: Frequent wear-and-tear parts (brake shoes, chain, sprocket, bulbs), engine oil, cheap tyres

Spending: RM30-100 per visit, very frequent (every 2-4 weeks for heavy riders)

How to tackle:

→ Cheapest price guarantee on basic parts — they compare every ringgit

→ Bulk oil deals — "Buy 4L get RM5 off" for monthly oil change

→ Payday promos — run deals on 25th-30th of each month

→ B2B: befriend the kilang — offer to be the "recommended shop" for factory staff. Put flyers in factory canteens

→ Credit book — trusted regulars can "hutang" and settle on payday (risky but builds loyalty)

🏎
Enthusiasts & Modders
High spend
▼

Smaller group but HIGHEST spend per customer

Profile: Age 18-35, passionate riders, active in FB groups & TikTok bike community. Often students or young workers

Bikes: Modified Y15ZR, RS150, Ninja 250, KTM Duke — want to stand out

Needs: Performance exhaust, racing sprockets, LED lights, carbon fiber parts, stickers, cosmetic mods

Spending: RM100-500+ per visit, willing to pay for cool factor

How to tackle:

→ Showcase wall — display modded parts in-store, let them touch/feel

→ TikTok "install" videos — show before/after of exhaust, LED, decal installs

→ Customer bike gallery — "Wall of Fame" photos of modded bikes (customers love this)

→ Group rides — sponsor/organize monthly Puncak Alam riders meetup

→ Pre-order rare parts — take orders for specialty items, charge deposit

🔧
Workshop Owners (B2B)
Bulk orders
▼

Lower margin but consistent high-volume orders

Profile: Small kedai motor in Puncak Alam, Ijok, Saujana Utama. 3-5 nearby workshops as regulars

Needs: Bulk brake pads, oil, filters, chains, sprockets — restocking fast-moving items

Spending: RM300-1,000 per order, 2-3x per week

How to tackle:

→ Trade pricing — 10-15% discount for registered workshop accounts

→ Free delivery — deliver within 10km for orders above RM200

→ Credit terms — net 14 days for trusted workshops

→ WhatsApp catalogue — send weekly stock list + new arrivals

→ Visit them personally — go workshop to workshop, introduce yourself, leave name card + price list
