## 7. TENANT'S OBLIGATIONS

The Tenant shall:

7.1 Pay the Rental on the due dates as stipulated in Clause 3 of this Agreement.

7.2 Pay all utility charges including electricity, water, telephone, internet, and any other services consumed at the Premises during the Tenancy Period.

7.3 Keep the Premises in good and tenantable repair and condition, fair wear and tear excepted.

7.4 Not make any structural alterations, additions, or improvements to the Premises without the prior written consent of the Landlord.

7.5 Not assign, sublet, or part with possession of the Premises or any part thereof without the prior written consent of the Landlord.

7.6 Permit the Landlord or the Landlord's authorized agent to enter and inspect the Premises at reasonable times upon prior notice.

7.7 Maintain the cleanliness and tidiness of the Premises and not cause or permit any nuisance, annoyance, or disturbance to neighboring properties.

7.8 Comply with all relevant fire safety regulations and maintain all fire safety equipment in proper working order.

7.9 Not store any hazardous, flammable, or illegal materials on the Premises.

7.10 Upon expiry or sooner determination of this tenancy, deliver up vacant possession of the Premises to the Landlord in good and clean condition, fair wear and tear excepted.

## 8. LANDLORD'S OBLIGATIONS

The Landlord shall:

8.1 Allow the Tenant quiet enjoyment of the Premises throughout the Tenancy Period without interruption, provided the Tenant duly observes and performs all the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

8.2 Be responsible for structural repairs to the Premises, unless caused by the Tenant's negligence or misuse.

8.3 Ensure that the Premises are in a fit and habitable condition for the Tenant's occupation at the commencement of the tenancy.

8.4 Pay all quit rent (cukai tanah) and assessment (cukai pintu/cukai taksiran) in respect of the Premises during the Tenancy Period.

10.2 **By Tenant:** The Tenant may terminate this Agreement by giving **two (2) months'** written notice to the Landlord, provided the Tenant has fulfilled all obligations up to the date of termination.

10.3 **Mutual Agreement:** Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time by mutual written agreement.
